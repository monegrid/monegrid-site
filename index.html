About Monegrid

Monegrid (Monemi Grid Technologies) is building AI for grid operators and campus microgrids. Our stack combines digital twins (RTDS/RSCAD), Pandapower simulations, and PyTorch models to deliver early fault detection and load-aware control.

  • • Fault classification and early warning
  • • AI-driven load forecasting for data-center spikes
  • • Adaptive response recommendations
  • • FastAPI backend feeding a lightweight ops dashboard

SIGMA — Smart Infrastructure Grid Monitoring AI

SIGMA is our AI engine for microgrids: a data and simulation–driven pipeline that detects faults early, forecasts heavy loads, and recommends adaptive responses. It fuses RTDS/RSCAD digital-twin scenarios with Pandapower studies and real telemetry to train PyTorch models that are explainable and operations-friendly.

Data & Digital Twin

RTDS/RSCAD cases + Pandapower sweeps → labeled events (faults, switching, load spikes) and healthy baselines.

ML Models

PyTorch classification & forecasting with feature audits, thresholds, and uncertainty checks.

Ops Outputs

FastAPI endpoints for alerts, recommended actions, and dashboard/SCADA integrations.
