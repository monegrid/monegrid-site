The grid has a bottleneck

The studies are the bottleneck. FERC Order 2023 and NERC PRC-029-1 mandate them in front of the meter; OSHA, NFPA 70E, and the NEC mandate them behind it. They cannot be skipped, and the specialists who run them are scarce — while every month a project sits in the queue costs its owner millions. That is the gap Monegrid was built to close.
