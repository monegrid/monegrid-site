About Monegrid

Monegrid (Monemi Grid Technologies) delivers AI for grid operators and campus microgrids. Our platform fuses digital-twin modeling with machine learning to detect faults early, anticipate heavy demand, and recommend operator-ready responses. The result: higher reliability, better asset utilization, and smoother operations.

  • • Early fault detection and event classification
  • • AI-driven load forecasting for heavy/AI/data-center profiles
  • • Adaptive response recommendations (operator-in-the-loop)
  • • FastAPI outputs ready for dashboards and alarms

SIGMA — Smart Infrastructure Grid Monitoring AI

SIGMA is our analytics engine: a data- and simulation-driven pipeline that detects faults early, forecasts heavy loads, and recommends adaptive responses. It blends RTDS/RSCAD digital-twin scenarios, Pandapower studies, and real telemetry to train robust PyTorch models — with outputs exposed via FastAPI for seamless operational use.

Data & Digital Twin

Synthetic and real datasets from RTDS/RSCAD and Pandapower create labeled events and healthy baselines.

ML Models

Classification & forecasting in PyTorch with feature audits, thresholds, and uncertainty checks.

Ops Outputs

FastAPI endpoints for alerts, recommended actions, and dashboard/SCADA integrations.
