What happens next

Three steps from first email to a deliverable you can audit line by line. No open-ended engagements, no black boxes.

01

15-minute discovery call

Mostly listening: your deadline, your queue position, your study backlog. If we are not the right fit, we say so on the call — not after a month of scoping.

02

Scoped, fixed proposal

A clearly bounded scope of work with deliverables, assumptions, and timeline named up front — so you know exactly what you are getting before you commit to anything.

03

A pilot study you can judge us on

Start with one study. Every assumption, setting, and result is auditable line by line — structured for licensed-engineer review. Decide on evidence, not promises.

Who should reach out

If you own, build, or maintain power infrastructure with a study standing between you and a deadline, that is us.

  • IBR fleet operators — solar, wind, and storage owner-operators facing PRC-029-1 ride-through deadlines.
  • Data-center & mission-critical facility teams — EMT studies to connect, arc-flash and coordination for your own gear.
  • EPC & MEP electrical design firms — overflow and white-label studies capacity when your bench is full.
  • Developers in the interconnection queue — projects waiting on studies that decide whether they ever energize.
  • Industrial & commercial facility owners — recurring NFPA 70E and NEC compliance studies, on a schedule you can plan around.
  • Inverter & turbine OEMs — PSCAD model validation ahead of FERC Order 2023 requirements.
